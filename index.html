About Grace Automotives

Grace Automotives was founded with a mission to deliver quality, trust, and comprehensive car care solutions. Inspired by the legacy of Late Er. Om Prakash Bhatia, we have grown to be a trusted car service provider in the region.

Vision

Building trust in every dealing and changing perceptions of car detailing with quality service and long-term relationships.

Mission

Delivering excellence by providing quality car care and comprehensive detailing solutions that matter to every customer.

Our Services

General Service

Comprehensive car servicing including full service, brakes, engine, and transmission repair.

Bodyshop

Dry denting, painting, bumper repair, and major/minor dent repair services.

Mechanical Repairs

Aluminum and plastic repairs, suspension replacement, check engine diagnostics, and more.

Electrical

Repair and replacement of alternators, batteries, ECM, BCM, and all accessory modules.

Air Conditioning

AC repair, gas recharge, airflow issues, and odor removal.

Detailing

Exterior and interior detailing, ceramic coating, PPF, rim detailing, and headlight restoration.
